Front End education by Artemij Fedosejev

Meet Artemij Fedosejev,
book author and tech instructor.

Artemij is building web products since the early 2000s. He earned his BSc in Computer Science from University College Cork, Ireland.

Artemij helped to grow a number of tech start-ups in London, UK and worked with JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, HTML5, CSS3, and other modern web technologies.

Artemij took leadership in creating front end for a couple of web applications in the public health space at Imperial College London. He played a key role in creating front end architecture using React.js and Flux for WGSA.net and Microreact.org.
